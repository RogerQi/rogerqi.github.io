Selected Research & Projects

My research interests lie in the intersection of robotics and computer vision, where I develop robotics perception algorithms that continually learn in an open world and are downstream-aware (i.e., efficient and planner-friendly for mobile robots) to solve fundamental challenges in creating embodied robot agents.
